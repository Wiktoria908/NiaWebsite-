Introduction

Nia is a mind/body physical conditioning program that initially stood for Non-Impact Aerobics, a health and fitness alternative that emerged in the '80's, and evolved to include neurological integrative practices and teachings.

Why choose Nia

With Nia you have the ability to use music and movement, and even the sound of our own voices, to help get you moving out of the mental noise that is so overwhelming and exhausting. Nia gives you the opportunity to shake up the nervous system so that that the body can release stress, tension, and anxiety. This means that a person can get out of their head, and down into their body, and from there they can make real progress with calming their nervous system.
