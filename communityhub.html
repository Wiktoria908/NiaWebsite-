Happy New year!!! 13 years ago I stepped into the brand new Dance House studios to start up my Monday evening class. Will you join me to dance my way into the class's third decade? I would love to have you!
New year's resolution to find fitness and overall well-being? Nia is the perfect solution. It's fun, different, both intense and gentle (you decide!) whole body fitness. Pain is NOT gain: be kind to yourself in 2020!
Want to taste it before you commit? Dance House is hosting their evening of free taster classes this Wednesday 8th January. Nia is at 7pm (link in comments).
Regular Monday evening Nia class 6:30-7:30 begins Monday 13th January. Dance House, Foley Street, Dublin 1 (off of Talbot Street and near Connolly Station). No classes bank holiday.
Ready to get moving?

News

Hi all, due to the new measures announced by the government today (closing schools, institutions, gatherings of more than 100 people), Dance house has closed all public classes until the 3rd of April. That is also the end of term one, so after the Easter holidays, term two will begin on Monday 20th April, all going well.
I recommend you regularly check the Dance Ireland website and facebook page for their latest news during this time. For any concerns or the latest information about Coronavirus in general, please refer to the HSE website:
hse.ie
For class card holders, there is no expiry date, so you can use them at any time. and I'll see you back for the new term.
I will miss our regular Monday classes but look forward to dancing next month in healthier times. For anyone looking to practice Nia during that time, NiaTV is an option! Check out https://www.niatv.fit/
Stay healthy and keep dancing!
