What Is Move IT? (Class 1 )

Nia's Move IT class uses the 52 Nia moves to deliver whole-body conditioning in just 60 minutes! This particular class integrates science, modern interval training, and music in order to provide the best outcome for you mentally and physically.

What is Classic Nia? (Class 2)

Classic Nia is the mixture of martial arts, dance and movement workout. The aim of Classic Nia is to leave you feeling spiritually energized, physically well and mentally clear in 60 minutes. We here practice classic Nia barefoot and non-impact

What is Free Dance? (Class 3)

Nia Free Dance is a programme designed to awaken your inner dancer. In this conscious dance experience you are guided o explore your own movement and experiment with your own form of expressive, creative dance. Through this freeing and mindful dance form you will learn how to intentionally craft multidimensional (that is body-emotion-mind-spirit) creativity.

What is Moving to Heal? (Class 4)

Moving to Heal is a softer type of Nia exercise,for people who have a wide range of therapeutic issues including, trauma, short-term and long-term illness and injury. It can assist with improving your well-being without being to challenging or tiresome.
